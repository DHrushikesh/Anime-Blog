HAIKYUU!!

My Most Favorite Sports anime which I rewatched more than 10 times. It has around 4 seasons and 4 movies



  • Plot Overview: "Haikyuu!" follows Hinata Shoyo, a determined high school student with dreams of becoming a volleyball champion despite his short stature.
  • Main Character: Hinata Shoyo, fueled by passion and unwavering spirit, joins his school's volleyball team.
  • Team Dynamics: Hinata forms unlikely friendships within the team as they strive to compete at the national level.
  • Exhilarating Matches: The series features thrilling volleyball matches that keep viewers on the edge of their seats.
  • Character Development: Each character undergoes significant growth and development throughout the series.
  • Themes: "Haikyuu!" explores themes of teamwork, perseverance, and determination.
  • Audience Appeal: The series appeals to both sports enthusiasts and anime fans alike.
  • Uplifting: Viewers are inspired by the series' uplifting portrayal of chasing dreams and overcoming challenges.

[ Naruto ]

My Most Favorite Action / Adventure anime with which I started watching anime is Naruto.



  • Emotional Rollercoaster: Naruto offers a journey filled with a range of emotions, from joy to sorrow, keeping viewers engaged throughout.
  • Diverse and Relatable Characters: The characters in Naruto are diverse, each with their own struggles and dreams, making them relatable to audiences.
  • Rooting for Naruto: Viewers will find themselves rooting for Naruto, the underdog ninja with big dreams, as he grows and overcomes obstacles.
  • Epic Action: The series features epic battles between ninjas and mind-blowing jutsu techniques, keeping the audience on the edge of their seats with adrenaline-pumping moments.
  • Valuable Life Lessons: Naruto teaches important life lessons about friendship, perseverance, and never giving up on dreams, inspiring viewers to reflect on their own journey in life.
  • Rich World-Building: The world of Naruto is rich in lore, exploring the history of ninja villages and unraveling mysteries as the story progresses, drawing viewers into its intricacies.

[ One Piece ]

Best Adventure Emotional Anime with more than 1200 Episodes And many arc more than 1 billion fans all over world is One Piece .



  • Captivating Storyline: Follow Luffy, a young pirate aiming to find a legendary treasure and become the Pirate King, on an exciting journey filled with surprises and thrilling adventures.
  • Unique and Fun Characters: Meet a diverse cast of characters, each with their own quirks and personalities, making every episode enjoyable and entertaining.
  • Exciting Adventures: Join Luffy and his crew as they embark on exhilarating adventures, encountering challenges and obstacles along the way that keep viewers engaged and eager for more.
  • Action, Humor, and Heartfelt Moments: "One Piece" offers a perfect blend of action-packed sequences, hilarious moments, and touching scenes, catering to a wide range of emotions and interests.
  • Accessible to All Audiences: Even if you're not typically into anime, "One Piece" has something for everyone, making it accessible and enjoyable for viewers of all ages and preferences.
  • Addictive and Engaging: Once you give it a try, you'll likely find yourself hooked on the series, unable to resist the allure of its captivating storyline and lovable characters.



[ Black Clover ]

Most Underrated Anime According to me , its the most soothing and very well written anime.



  • Unique Protagonist and Setting: Follow Asta, a boy without magic in a world where magic is prevalent, as he strives to become the Wizard King, defying the odds and pursuing his dreams in a captivating setting.
  • Inspiring Characters: Encounter a cast of characters who inspire viewers with their determination, resilience, and camaraderie, adding depth and richness to the story.
  • Exciting Action Scenes: Experience heart-pounding action as Asta and his allies engage in intense battles, showcasing their skills and powers in thrilling showdowns.
  • Emotional Depth: "Black Clover" offers more than just action; it delves into themes of friendship, perseverance, and self-discovery, creating heartfelt moments that resonate with viewers.
  • Accessible to Newcomers: Even for those new to anime, "Black Clover" is easy to get into, thanks to its engaging storyline, dynamic characters, and fast-paced action, making it a welcoming entry point to the genre.
  • Addictive and Enjoyable: Once you start watching "Black Clover," you'll likely find yourself hooked on its captivating story and eager to follow Asta's journey to its exciting conclusion.





[ JuJutsu Kaisen ]

Best Adaptation of manga



  • Unique Concept: The series introduces a captivating premise where cursed energy and sorcery collide, setting the stage for intense battles and supernatural encounters.
  • Complex Characters: Dive into a world populated by diverse and well-developed characters, each with their own motivations, backstories, and powers, adding depth and richness to the story.
  • Action-packed Battles: Brace yourself for adrenaline-pumping action scenes filled with dynamic animation and creative use of cursed techniques, keeping viewers at the edge of their seats.
  • Dark Atmosphere: "Jujutsu Kaisen" masterfully blends elements of horror, mystery, and supernatural, creating a dark and immersive atmosphere that adds to the suspense and intrigue of the plot.
  • Emotional Depth: Beyond the action, the series explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the consequences of wielding immense power, resonating with viewers on an emotional level.
  • Accessible and Addictive: Whether you're a seasoned anime fan or new to the genre, "Jujutsu Kaisen" offers a compelling and accessible story that hooks viewers from the very first episode, making it a must-watch for anyone seeking thrilling and engaging entertainment.
